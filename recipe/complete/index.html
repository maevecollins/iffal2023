• "Try-and-Guess" Salad

    Serves 12. This sounds perfectly horrible but it is delicious. Guests, after raving, always try and guess the ingredients - hence the name. You won't need to worry about leftovers! The recipe only takes about 10 minutes to prepare, but will need to chill for 2-4 hours.

  • INGREDIENTS

    3 (3-oz.) pkgs. raspberry jello
    1 1/4 cup hot water
    3 cans (1lb each) stewed tomatoes
    6 drops Tabasco sauce
    Mixed salad greens
    1 pt sour cream
    1 T. creamed horseradish
    1/4 tsp salt
    1/2 tsp sugar

  • TOOLS

    Ring Mold
    Refrigerator
    Measuring cups
    Measuring spoons

  • PREPARATION

    Dissolve raspberry gelatin in hot water.
    Stir in stewed tomatoes, breaking the tomatoes with a spoon.
    Add Tabasco sauce.
    Pour into lightly oiled 3-quart ring mold.
    Chill until firm. Unmold onto greens.
    Fill center with sour cream which has been added to horseradish, salt and sugar.
